Example 01 — Workflow Assessment

Mapping where a brand's product creation process was breaking down

A mid-size brand with a 35-person product team. Four tools in use, none connected. We ran a 3-week assessment and mapped every handoff, delay, and duplication in the process.

Workflow map image

What the assessment found

14 manual handoffs identified

Between design, development, and buying — each requiring a person to move or re-enter information.

3 critical duplication points

The same product data was being entered in three separate systems with no single source of truth.

Priority fix identified

One integration between their PLM and supplier portal would eliminate 60% of the manual work. We scoped it in week three.

Deliverable

Full workflow map · friction log · prioritised fix list · integration scoping brief

Example 02 — Automation

Automating the handoff from design approval to supplier brief

When a product is approved in CLO, the brief used to be assembled manually — pulling specs, images, and colourways from three places before emailing a supplier. We automated the entire handoff.
