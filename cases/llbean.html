How L.L.Bean went from manual file handling to a fully connected 3D workflow

CLO  ·  VibeIQ  ·  Workflow Automation

L.L.Bean had invested heavily in Digital Product Creation. The 3D capability was strong. But as adoption scaled, a quieter problem emerged — designers were spending significant time on work that wasn't design.

Renaming files. Re-exporting assets. Moving content between tools. Small tasks, but constant ones. Consistency suffered. Confidence in what was flowing into VibeIQ eroded.

"There were a lot of little disconnects in the workflow that slowed designers down."

Digital Product Creation Lead, L.L.Bean

The Challenge

Scaling 3D meant the manual overhead scaled with it

Scaling 3D across more categories meant the manual overhead scaled with it. File sizes grew. Handoffs multiplied. Most delays weren't coming from rendering — they were coming from everything around it.

Designers lost momentum switching between tasks. Leadership couldn't trust that the right assets were in VibeIQ at the right time. The workflow needed to change.

What We Found

The time study surfaced what everyone suspected but hadn't quantified

EnhanceThat ran a workflow assessment with L.L.Bean's Digital Product Creation and Product teams. Saving, renaming, re-exporting and moving files was consuming far more time than rendering itself.

The cost wasn't just hours. Every context switch broke creative momentum. The case for automation was immediate.

The Approach

One pipeline. Built around how the team actually works.

EnhanceThat built a seamless pipeline connecting CLO, product data and VibeIQ — designed around how L.L.Bean's designers and product teams actually work.

Manual handoffs were removed. Naming, exporting and syncing happen automatically. The workflow is fully implemented within L.L.Bean's environment and built to evolve as tools and requirements change. EnhanceThat continues to support it over time.

From the L.L.Bean workflow study. Most delays came from saving, naming, re-exporting and manual handovers between tools, rather than from rendering itself.

The Impact

Numbers that changed the conversation

  • From 5+ minutes to 16 seconds — 95% faster
  • High quality images in 7 clicks — 84% less clicking
  • Designers spend more time designing, less time managing files
  • Assets are consistent across the workflow, with a shared visual language
  • VibeIQ receives clean, structured data and the latest 3D assets
  • Rework drops. Confidence in the pipeline goes up.

"Removing manual handovers helped our designers stay focused on design rather than file management."

Digital Product Creation Lead, L.L.Bean

Everyone spoke the same language from day one

The team immediately understood both the workflow and the constraints of scaling Digital Product Creation. No lengthy briefing process. Everyone spoke the same language — designers, DPC, Tech, CLO and VibeIQ. EnhanceThat took ownership of the integration end to end, removing coordination overhead for L.L.Bean's internal teams.

What It Unlocked

  • Manual file handling removed from the core 3D workflow
  • Disconnected tools now run as a single reliable pipeline

  • Digital Product Creation is positioned to scale across every category
